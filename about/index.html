Suyoung (Mel) Kwon

“I’m a Product Manager dedicated to bridging the gap between
people and technology to deliver impact where it is needed most.”

I am a second-year Master’s student in Information Management with a minor in Data Science at KAIST. Prior to my graduate studies, I led enterprise-wide AI Transformation projects as an AI Product Manager at Kurly, an e-commerce unicorn in Korea. I also served as a PM and cross-functional team lead at Asleep, an AI sleep-tech startup, where I managed the 0-to-1 growth of products designed to improve sleep quality.

As a visiting scholar at Carnegie Mellon University SCS, I was fortunate to work with Professors Rita Singh and Bhiksha Raj at the Language Technologies Institute. I also worked as a research assistant for Professor Seohyun Jung at KAIST HSS, exploring metaverse environments and narrative theory.

My professional philosophy is deeply rooted in Human-Centered Design, which I cultivated during my undergraduate studies in Industrial Design (UX/UI) at Kookmin University. I am passionate about translating complex data and technology into sustainable, global solutions that fundamentally address challenges in healthcare and mental health.
